About YouTbooks

Transforming manuscripts into professional books since 2015

Our Story

2015 - Foundation

YouTbooks was founded by a team of publishing professionals who saw a gap in the market for high-quality book design services for independent authors.

"We started with a simple mission: to help authors create books that could compete with traditionally published titles in terms of quality and design."

- Sarah Williams, Founder

"Expanding our team allowed us to take on more complex projects and offer specialized services for different book genres."

- Michael Chen, Creative Director

2017 - Expansion

We expanded our team to include specialized designers for different book genres and added professional editing services to our offerings.

2019 - Digital Focus

As eBooks became increasingly popular, we developed specialized formatting services to ensure our clients' books looked great on all digital platforms.

"The digital revolution changed publishing forever. We made sure our authors were at the forefront of this change with beautifully formatted eBooks."

- Jessica Taylor, Digital Director

"Adding illustration services was a natural evolution. Now we can help children's book authors and educational publishers bring their stories to life visually."

- David Rodriguez, Illustration Lead

2021 - Illustration Department

We launched our illustration department to serve children's book authors and educational publishers, offering custom illustrations that bring stories to life.

2023 - Today

Today, YouTbooks is a full-service book design agency serving authors worldwide, with a reputation for quality, creativity, and exceptional customer service.

"Looking back at our journey, I'm proud of how we've grown while staying true to our mission of helping authors succeed with beautiful books."

- Sarah Williams, Founder

Our Mission

At YouTbooks, our mission is to empower authors to create professional, beautiful books that stand out in today's competitive market. We believe that every author deserves access to high-quality design services, regardless of whether they're publishing traditionally or independently.

We're committed to combining artistic excellence with technical expertise to create books that authors are proud to share with the world.

Our Vision

We envision a publishing landscape where independent authors have the same quality resources as traditional publishers. Our goal is to be the premier design partner for authors worldwide, known for our creativity, reliability, and exceptional customer service.

We strive to continuously innovate and adapt to the evolving publishing industry, ensuring our clients' books remain competitive and relevant in both print and digital formats.
