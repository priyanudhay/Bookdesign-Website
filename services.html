Our Services

Comprehensive book design services to transform your manuscript into a professional, market-ready book.

Professional Editing

Our editing services ensure your manuscript is polished, error-free, and ready for publication. We offer different levels of editing to meet your specific needs:

  • Developmental Editing: Comprehensive feedback on structure, plot, character development, and overall narrative.
  • Copyediting: Correction of grammar, spelling, punctuation, and consistency issues while preserving your unique voice.
  • Proofreading: Final review to catch any remaining errors before publication.

Print Interior Layout

We create beautiful, professional interior layouts that enhance readability and reader experience. Our interior design services include:

  • Custom Typography: Selection of appropriate fonts and typesetting for your genre and audience.
  • Professional Formatting: Proper margins, spacing, headers, footers, page numbers, and chapter headings.
  • Print-Ready Files: Optimized for KDP, IngramSpark, and other print-on-demand services.
  • Special Elements: Tables, charts, images, and other visual elements properly integrated.

eBook Formatting

We convert your manuscript into professional, device-friendly eBook formats that look great on all e-readers. Our eBook services include:

  • Multiple Formats: EPUB, MOBI, and other formats for all major platforms.
  • Responsive Design: Content that adapts to different screen sizes and devices.
  • Interactive Elements: Hyperlinked table of contents, footnotes, and external links.
  • Quality Assurance: Testing on multiple devices to ensure consistent display.
